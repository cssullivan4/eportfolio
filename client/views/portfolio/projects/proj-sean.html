#PROMOTIONAL SITE

>>> ASK

Create promotional site narrow in scope that can be utilized with mouse, track pad, arrow keys, and touch while being optimized across screen sizes.

>>> TIMEFRAME

5-day build from scratch.

>>> APPROACH

The challenge beyond building & deploying within a week's time was deciding the design direction as the client's industry does not lean on promotional sites for work; there were few examples to look towards or show client. Deciding to narrow the scope to 1) seeking work and 2) on a campaign, and after few phone calls & email exchanges with the client, the site came together quite fast.

>>> FEATURES

  • Full-page slides
  • Mouse, track pad, arrow keys, touch to maneuver site
  • Contact & connection buttons
  • Custom design, including vector portrait, Parthenon, TN flag, and different states.

>>> TECHNOLOGIES

  • AngularJS, ExpressJS, jQuery, NodeJS, Bootstrap, Fullpage.js, Amazon Web Services, Figma
