#CSAM&.ME

WHAT:
All icons and graphics created for and used by this web app.
WHY:
I'm a visual person, and I believe navigation or wayfinding can better be said in pictures, not words.
HOW:
When I come to a place where I think a need is found, I brainstorm what it is I want to convey. I'll write down a few words, then I will sketch out some designs. I'll take it to Sketch or Figma, and begin shaping from there.
