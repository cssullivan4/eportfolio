#STATE

WHAT:
Rubber relief and prints of the state of Tennessee
WHY:
Commission for simple postcards grew into creating permanent stamp. 4 other states + 1 continent are not listed below.
HOW:
Began with pencil drawing of state, then inked onto trace paper. Eventually took to rubber stamp where carved out.
WHEN:
2015

#MISCELLEANEOUS
