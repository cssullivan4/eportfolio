WEB DESIGN & ME

Always the painter, I first dove into the web design (and development) space in 2003 with my first computer. From virtual pet websites, using such programs as Microsoft Paint, Jasc Paint Shop Pro 9, Animation Shop 3, I found myself creating images and animations for myself and others.

I returned from a hiatus in web design (and development) towards the end of my high school career in 2008, stumbling across SWiSHMAX. I created interactive programs for myself, my friends, and eventually for the public with what became an unfinished, unreleased (publicly) flash-based RPG game built entirely on the platform.

The galleries shown here are a few selections of mostly dated - but relevant - pieces of work. They are instrumental of who I am and from where I come. Being said, the pieces are not taken so seriously or as far as what they are: juvenile works playing & learning within the realms of web & graphic design throughout the 2000s.

Please enjoy, and please look forward to more adult work being uploaded momentarily.
