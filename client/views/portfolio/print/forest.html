#WOODLAND ANIMALS

WHAT:
Series of Woodland Animals
WHY:
The series started to explore the growing phenomenon at-the-time of woodland animals becoming graphically trendy (owls and foxes, most notably).
HOW:
Either the idea of the look of the animal or the color scheme started the page, but pencil was applied first, followed by watercolor and ink. There's supposed to be a connectioned between the expression and their set.
WHEN:
2013
