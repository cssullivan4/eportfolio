#SPHERE

WHAT:
Simple balance concept, oil + ink, only substitute oil with watercolor.
WHY:
Challenge to explore how watercolor interacts with an unfriendly surface. Chose to experiment further by creating a series of 3, pulling major elements (fire, earth, water, air) and a reductive taken on biosphere (hydrosphere, troposphere, stratosphere).
HOW:
Cut down on water ratio to mostly apply pigments, which had the last stroke impact. Used higher ratio of water after paint had dried to see how much of the design can be controlled.
WHEN:
2013
