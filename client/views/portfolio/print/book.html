#BOOK

WHAT:
Cocunut fibers book filled with unique animals on every page.
WHY:
Project began as way to play with the interpretation of what it means to be an artist. One attribute has been described as "seeing faces," and taking this into consideration, each page is created only from what face I see within the pulp-paper. Challenge is to bring what is seen in the fibers to life.
HOW:
Sketch out faces when see them. Watercolor and ink to bring to life. Examples below of each.
WHEN:
2013 - 2016 (project on-going)
