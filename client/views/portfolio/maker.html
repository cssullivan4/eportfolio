MAKERISM & ME

I come from a family of makers. Both sides of my family have deep immigrant roots, which give to the inherited tradition of understanding the workings of - virtually - anything & everything, and then making it yourself, often through improvisation.

I practiced many of these things as a child - sewing, wood working, sculpting, robotics - and recently spent time in a dedicated maker space leading daily programming and special instruction. A few selected projects will be rolled out populating this field soon.
