#DISCLAIMER

"Politi" is my reference to the space relating to all things public policy, elected office, and activism, and it is where I have increasingly found myself. I've been drawn towards the policy side for years, but I have recently turned an eye towards action, like held office or organizing.

There is caution to be had with being open about a penchant towards the political space, but as can be inferred from the contents of this site, my politics aren't overt. The draw for me is either socially motivated singularly or the draw is motivated by problem-solving (I enjoy development, systems, & contingencies). It is less motivated by self or party.
