I have found myself increasinly in the "politi" space, as I've been referring to public policy, elected office, and activism. I've been drawn towards the policy side for years (refer to /Aspiring Social Entrepreneur), but I have recently turned an eye towards action, like held office or organizing. From SGA at University to finding myself interning at the Mayor's to joining the political sphere, I can no longer deny this force in my life. There is caution to be had with being open about a penchant towards the political space, but as can be inferred from the contents of this site, my politics aren't overt. The draw for me is socially motivated or it is motivated by problem-solving (I enjoy development, systems, contingencies), and it is less motivated by self or party.
